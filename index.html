Generative AI-powered diagnostic system for early-stage breast cancer using ultrasound imaging. Combined GANs, Stable Diffusion, and hybrid deep learning (ResNet50 + XGBoost) to achieve 95% accuracy, matching experienced radiologists at 1/30th the time. Built and deployed an iOS app for frontline clinicians in low-resource settings.

GliSM
Aug 2024 – Present
Deep learning platform to identify novel GLS-1 inhibitors for aggressive cancers (glioblastoma, pancreatic cancer, triple-negative breast cancer). Screened 10M compounds using PubChem10 + ChemBERTa transformers with ensemble hybrid learning. Validated with AutoDockVina 3D docking. Reduces early-stage drug discovery from 5-6 years of manual screening.
